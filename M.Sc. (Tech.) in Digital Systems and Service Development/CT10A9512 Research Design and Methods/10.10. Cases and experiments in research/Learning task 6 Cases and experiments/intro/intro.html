Invent a very short example (just a title) of the following types of case studies:
  • Exploratory - finding out what is happening, seeking new insights and generating ideas and hypotheses for new research
  • Descriptive - portraying a situation or phenomenon
  • Explanatory - seeking an explanation of a situation or a problem, mostly in the form of a causal relationship
  • Improving/emancipatory - trying to improve a certain aspect of the studied phenomenon
